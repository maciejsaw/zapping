Who created Zapping?
Hi, I am Maciej Sawicki. I work as a User Experience designer. I experiment in Javascript. Learn more about me on my website or my linkedin profile.
What problem does Zapping solve?
Choosing what to watch is difficult. Filtering YouTube for interesting content is even more tiring. 
How does it solve this problem?
Zapping was inspired by a classic TV experience:
  • You just turn it on and watch
  • You can switch channel instantly
  • You start watching in the middle of a program, you skip boring intro screens
  • You don't need to decide for a specific video
How is it better than regular TV?
Zapping immitates classic TV experience, but there are a few advantages:
  • Zapping has different content. You can watch your favourite YouTubers here.
  • You can watch it on your Android mobile phone and laptop
  • You can rewind any video, even if you just started watching a channel
Who chooses channels content?
I edit channels content manually. I try to choose only videos with interesting content.
Can I join this project?
Sure, just send me an email. You can also read more technical details on GitHub project page
